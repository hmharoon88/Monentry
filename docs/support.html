Getting started

Log an expense: Tap − Expense on the Today tab, enter the amount, pick a type, and save.

Log income: Tap + Income the same way.

See monthly totals: Open the Summary tab.

Delete entries

One entry: Long-press a transaction on Today or Summary → Delete.

Everything: Me tab → Clear all data.

Dark mode

Me tab → Appearance → choose System, Light, or Dark.

Data & privacy

Monentry v1.0 stores data only on your device. No account is required. Read our Privacy Policy.
