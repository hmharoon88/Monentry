Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Overview

Monentry ("we," "our," or "us") is a simple expense and income tracker. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how Monentry handles your information.

Data stored on your device

Monentry stores expense and income entries locally on your device using on-device storage (SQLite). When you sign in, entries are also backed up to the cloud.

  • Expense and income amounts, categories, and optional tags
  • Theme preference (light, dark, or system)

Cloud backup

When you create a free Monentry account, your expense and income entries are saved to Google Firebase (Firestore) so you can restore them on a new phone or device.

  • Account email address (for sign-in)
  • Your transaction entries (amounts, categories, tags, dates)

Sign in on any device to download your latest data. Without an account, data stays on your device only and is lost if you uninstall the app or change phones.

What we do not collect

  • No bank account linking
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party trackers
  • No sale of personal data

Data you control

You can delete individual entries (long-press) or clear all data from the Me tab. Clearing data removes entries on your device and, if cloud sync is enabled, marks them deleted in your cloud backup. Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device.

Third-party services

Monentry Plus uses Google Firebase for authentication and cloud storage. Firebase processes data according to Google's privacy policy.

Children's privacy

Monentry is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
